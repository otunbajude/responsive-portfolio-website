I am a dad to a beautiful daughter (Valerie Adenuga). After enjoying over a decade of industry work experience, I am on track to complete a Doctorate in Instructional Technology & Design at the American College of Education (ACE) in 2023. My qualifications include a Master of Arts in Technology and Learning Design from the University of Roehampton (UK), a Master of Education in Globalization from Moreland University (USA), and a Bachelor of Science in Computing from the University of Greenwich (UK). Although my passion for technology started in childhood, it was during my Bachelorâ€™s degree that I developed my first 3-tier desktop application. While it was a basic hospital information management system that performed CRUD (create, read, update, and delete) operations, the experience exposed me to the problem-solving opportunities that computing offers. This instilled in me the passion to continue to research and teach computing through the lens of design and information technology. My journey as an instructional designer & information systems engineer has made me passionate about educating aspiring engineers in their pursuit to be problem-solvers. I help students to develop the ability to apply evidence-based algorithm and design strategies to solve complex real-life problems. I am experienced at designing courses that are accessible to all learners regardless of their readiness level or prior experience of the taught content. I have taught Design, Comp Science, ICT, and ESL to high-schoolers and college students.
